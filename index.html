MY PROJECTS

Zaika(Sense of Taste) Recipes is a Recipe app! The Idea for this project started with a problem that i have been facing. I love cooking for my family and friends, and while i'm cooking i experiment alot and when things turn out really well i wasnt be able to save those recipes to come back to. So, when my Husband loved the recipe i made few months ago, i wasn't able to make it exactly the same way again, because i didn't have a way of saving the recipes easily, while i was making it! Which is why i made this app Zaika Recipes. It has a record of all the users recipes and also be able to plan meals. And I have built this Project in 2 weeks.

Technologies i have used React, Redux, node.js, Express, material UI for styling and Nodemailer to send emails.

Checkout my App: Zaika Recipes
